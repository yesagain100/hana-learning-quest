Privacy Policy — Hana's Learning Quest

Last updated: July 2026

In one sentence: Hana's Learning Quest does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your child. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not ask for a name, email, location, or any personal detail. It does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party trackers.

Learning progress

Your child's stars, XP, completed lessons, and chosen voice settings are saved only on the device itself (in the browser's local storage / the app's own storage). This information never leaves the device and is never sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Internet & permissions

The app works fully offline — all lessons, stories, and voices are included inside the app. It does not require an internet connection to run and makes no calls to any server for its core features. It does not request access to your microphone, camera, contacts, or photos in the App Store version.

Children's privacy

This app is designed for children and complies with child-privacy principles by collecting no data at all. Because nothing is collected, there is nothing to disclose, sell, or delete on request.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
